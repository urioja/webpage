Unai Rioja

Ph.D. student at Ikerlan Technological Research Center and Radboud University

I am an external Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Lejla Batina in the Digital Security group of the Radboud University in Nijmegen, The Netherlands. I am also part of Ikerlan, a Technological Research Center located in the Basque Country (Spain), where I reside. Concretely, I am part of the Cybersecurity of Embedded Systems team of Ikerlan, where I work as a researcher (Ph.D. student). My main research interests are in the area of analysis, design, and evaluation of Embedded Systems and mainly focus on Cybersecurity for embedded systems and Side-Channel Attacks.
